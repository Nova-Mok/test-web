2. Payment Terms

Payments are processed securely through our payment partners. We accept:

  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)

3. Billing Cycle

Monthly subscriptions are billed on the same date each month. Annual subscriptions are billed annually with a 20% discount. All payments are due in advance.

4. Price Changes

We reserve the right to modify our pricing. Existing subscribers will be notified 30 days in advance of any price changes. Changes will take effect on your next billing cycle.

5. Taxes

All prices include applicable taxes as per Indian tax laws. GST will be charged as per current rates and reflected in your invoice.

6. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No partial refunds are provided for unused portions of your subscription.

7. Refund Policy

Refund requests must be submitted within 15 days of purchase to support@pitchpad.in. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

8. Failed Payments

If a payment fails, we will attempt to process it again within 3 days. If payment continues to fail, your account may be suspended until payment is resolved.

9. Disputes

For billing disputes, contact us at support@pitchpad.in within 60 days of the charge. We will investigate and resolve disputes promptly.
